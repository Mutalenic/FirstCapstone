Hello! African Hackers

Hackers In Residence Africa Conference 2022

The Hackers In Residence Conference is an annual invitation-only gathering of designers, engineers and programmers to discuss the latest developments and innovations in the computer industry. On a daily basis, many hackers only interact virtually, and therefore rarely have face-to-face contact. The conference is a time for hackers to come together to share ideas in Nkwashi city of Zambia.

2022-07-28(THU)~30(FRI)

@ Nkwashi city Hall ,Lusaka , Zambia.

Main Program

Lecture

Listen to speakers from various countries in Africa and learn about the advantages of Hackers in residence solving real world problems.

CC Exhibition

Let's meet innovators from the the Tech industry who share their technogies to address challenges that Africa is facing.

Forum

It's the time to share our opinions and thoughts with experts about doing business in the new normal.

Workshop

Use your abilities and imagination to make creations by using your hand in open source.

CC Ignite

Create opportunities to freely network with new normal business around Africa.
